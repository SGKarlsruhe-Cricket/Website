About SG Karlsruhe Cricket

Formed by community cricket enthusiasts, SG Karlsruhe Cricket is growing cricket through training, matches, youth development and volunteer support.

Our Vision

To develop young players with strong character on and off the field by providing world-class cricket practice facilities in South Germany.

Our Mission

To celebrate the sport and unite cricket communities — bringing children, parents, beginners and experienced players together through cricket.

Junior Cricket

Our junior program helps children learn cricket fundamentals, teamwork and confidence through fun training sessions and match-day experiences.

U11 Winter League 2025

SG Karlsruhe hosted a six-team U11 Winter League and received excellent feedback from visiting teams for raising the bar as hosts.

Strong U11 Performance

Our U11 team finished 3rd overall in a strong field, supported by coaches and youth umpires Sherwin, Parth and Vihaan.

Player Achievements

SGK players featured strongly in the batting and bowling rankings, including best batsman Adith Madathil, two SGK batsmen in the top 3 and three SGK bowlers in the top 3.

Facilities & Future Plans

We already have useful training resources — and a clear ambition to build one of the best cricket practice environments in Southern Germany.

Current Facilities

  • Cricket bats, balls and stumps
  • Bowling machine
  • Hall 1 and Hall 2 at SGK
  • Football ground usage in summer
  • Access to a fully-equipped kitchen for catering needs

Future Aspiration

Develop cricket practice bays with netting and work towards state-of-the-art cricket practice facilities in South Germany.

2025 Highlights

2025 gave SGK Cricket important momentum through hosting, youth development, visibility and community involvement.

U11 Winter League Host

Hosted six teams, created a strong match-day environment and received excellent feedback from guest teams.

Visibility for SGK Cricket

The Mayor’s Office Vorstand attended, and BNN coverage helped increase visibility of SGK Cricket.
