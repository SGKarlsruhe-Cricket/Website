About SG Karlsruhe Cricket

SG Karlsruhe Cricket was formed by community cricket enthusiasts and continues to grow through training, matches, youth development and volunteer support.

Our Vision

To develop young players with strong character on and off the field by providing excellent cricket practice facilities in South Germany.

Our Mission

To celebrate the sport and unite cricket communities — bringing children, parents, beginners and experienced players together through cricket.

What we offer

  • Youth cricket and U11 development
  • Beginner-friendly cricket for new players
  • Softball cricket and transition to hard-ball cricket
  • Women cricket, friendly matches and tournaments
  • Volunteer opportunities for parents and community members

Facilities & Future Plans

We already have useful training resources — and a clear ambition to build one of the best cricket practice environments in Southern Germany.

Current Facilities

  • Cricket bats, balls and stumps
  • Bowling machine
  • Indoor hall access at SGK
  • Outdoor ground usage during the summer season
  • Kitchen access for catering and community events

Future Aspiration

Develop cricket practice bays with netting and work towards high-quality cricket practice facilities for youth and adult players in South Germany.
