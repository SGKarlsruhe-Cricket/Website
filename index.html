A friendly cricket department at SG Karlsruhe

We are building a welcoming cricket community in Karlsruhe — with a special focus on children, families, learning, teamwork and enjoying the sport together.

Our vision

To help young players develop strong character on and off the field, while creating excellent cricket opportunities in South Germany.

Our mission

Celebrate cricket and unite communities — from children trying the sport for the first time to adults who want to train, play and support the game locally.
